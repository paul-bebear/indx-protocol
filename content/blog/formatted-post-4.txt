ChatGPT isn't "smart" about restaurants. It doesn't taste your food. It doesn't visit your website. It doesn't judge your ambiance.

ChatGPT is a (BOLD) pattern-matching engine (END BOLD) that works with whatever data it has access to.

How AI Actually Makes Recommendations

(H3)
The Confidence Score System

When a customer asks for a recommendation, AI assigns confidence scores:

(BOLD) High Confidence Factors (+0.2 each): (END BOLD)
✓ Schema markup present
✓ Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
✓ Machine-readable menu
✓ FAQ content
✓ Recent activity

(BOLD) Low Confidence Factors (-0.15 each): (END BOLD)
✗ PDF menu
✗ Image-only content
✗ Inconsistent information
✗ No schema markup

Most AI assistants use a threshold of 0.7 for recommendations.

• Score 0.7+: Included in recommendations
• Score <0.4: Invisible—never mentioned

Your competitor at 0.92 gets recommended. You at 0.3 don't exist.
