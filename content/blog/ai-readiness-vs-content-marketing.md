## Why Content Marketing Fails for Restaurants


### 1. Wrong Problem


Content marketing assumes your problem is "not enough content."

Your actual problem: AI assistants can't read your website.

Adding blog posts doesn't fix that. It's like adding books to a library with no catalog system.

### 2. Insane Competition


You're competing with national chains with $100K+ content budgets, food bloggers with millions of followers, and AI tools generating 1,000 articles per day.

> You cannot out-content the internet.


### 3. Wrong Target


58% of restaurant discovery now happens through AI assistants—not Google search. Blog posts don't help you get recommended by ChatGPT or Alexa.
