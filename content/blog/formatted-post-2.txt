(H2)
The Confidence Score

AI assistants use confidence scoring to determine recommendations. Here's what matters:

(H3)
High Confidence (+points):
✓ Schema markup present
✓ Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
✓ Machine-readable menu
✓ FAQ content
✓ Recent activity

(H3)
Low Confidence (-points):
✗ PDF menu
✗ Image-only content
✗ Inconsistent information
✗ No schema markup

Most AI assistants use a threshold of 0.7 for recommendations.

• Score 0.7+: Included in recommendations
• Score <0.4: Invisible—never mentioned

Your competitor at 0.92 gets recommended. You at 0.3 don't exist.
